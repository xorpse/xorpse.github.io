I am a Principal Research Scientist at BINARLY. Previously, I was a Research Fellow at the Centre for Cyber Security and Privacy (part of the University of Birmingham), and before that, I was Maître de conférences (equivalent to Assistant Professor) at CentraleSupélec within the CIDRE team. I completed my PhD on Backdoor Detection Systems for Embedded Devices under the joint supervision of Flavio Garcia and Tom Chothia at the University of Birmingham.
